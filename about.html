About Me

Hi, I’m Jillian McDonald, born and raised in Marietta, Georgia. I’m currently a third-year Information Technology student at Kennesaw State University. My passion for technology started at a young age and has only grown stronger as I’ve gained experience with various programming languages such as JavaScript, Python, HTML, and CSS.

I’ve spent the last few years honing my skills in web development, focusing on creating responsive and user-friendly websites. My goal is to work with startups after graduation, using technology to solve real-world problems.

In my spare time, I enjoy exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, and staying up-to-date with the latest trends in IT. I look forward to a future where I can contribute my knowledge and skills to make a positive impact in the tech world.

I’m also interested in artificial intelligence and machine learning, and I’ve recently started experimenting with integrating AI into web applications. It’s an exciting field with a lot of potential!
